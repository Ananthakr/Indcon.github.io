About Us

College of Engineering, Guindy is one of the premier institutes of technical education in India. Established in the year 1794, it boasts of illustrious history in training and grooming brilliant and talented young minds from across the country. Owing to its high educational standards and lavish infrastructure it is consistantsly ranked among top ten engineering colleges in India. It lays emphasis on organisational skills and encourages extra curricular activities which are essential for the wholesome development of the students.

College of Engineering, Guindy

The Department enters it's 34th year with the efforts and guidance of a highly deciated and experianced facutly. It aims at serving the society by transforming young minds into the thought leaders of tomorrow. Industrial Engineering is multi-disciplinary course that encompasses technical skills and management practices which makes an industrial engineer and invaluable part of any organisation. The alumni of our department are leaders in the field of system simulation, quality management, faculty planning etc.

Industrial Department

The Department of Industrial Engineering at the College of Engineering Guindy is proud to present INDCON 2016 - two day festival that witnesses the confluence of the greatest minds from across various engineering disciplines. It aims to provide a platform for students from various streams and colleges to interact and compete in various engineering events. With 12 events and Rs. 1 lakh cash prizes to be won, INDCON 2016 promises to be the most successful Industrial Engineering Symposium in the country.
